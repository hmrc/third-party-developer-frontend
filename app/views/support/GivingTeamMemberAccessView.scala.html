Adding team members to an application

  • Sign in to your developer hub account
  • Click the 'applications' link at the top of the page
  • Click the name of the application you want to add a team member to
  • Click 'Team members'
  • Enter the email address of the team member you would like to add to the application
  • Set their permissions to 'developer' or 'administrator'
  • Your new team member will receive an email confirming they have been added to the application
